Evolve Community

After completing the 16-weeks program you will be invited to join the EVOLVE community. Our members share a commitment to continuous personal growth and impact. We support each other on our life journeys through practicing inner and relational skills, listening with compassion, sharing reflections and inspiration. We provide a space for depth and lightness, for mind and heart.

The membership provides access to:

  1. Monthly guided 90-minute EVOLVE community zoom gatherings with inspirations, practices and personal sharing (for major life aspects (e.g. family, intimacy, time, money, health, purpose, etc.)
  2. Monthly challenge/practice in line with inspiration topic
  3. Intimate engagements with world leading topical experts in live online sessions
  4. Monthly structured practice group meetings (4 peers) to check in, practice and support each other
  5. Membership website with learning resources and ability to interact with the community
