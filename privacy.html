Privacy statement

These websites are offered by Evolve-Now Academy, Schaperstraße 19, 10719 Berlin, Germany, (“Evolve-Now Academy”, "us", "our" or "we"). For Evolve-Now Academy, your privacy and the protection of your personal data, i.e. any indication of personal or factual circumstances that you designate or make identifiable as a natural person, is of the highest priority while using our websites. We assure you that we will treat your personal data confidentially and in accordance with the statutory data protection regulations and this data protection declaration.

What personal data do we collect?

When you visit the Evolve-Now Academy websites, your web browser automatically transmits some information, such as the IP address of the device you are using, the web browser you are using, the operating system, the referrer URL (i.e. the URL from which you visit our websites) and the date and duration of your visit. This information is temporarily stored in the so-called log files on the server. This is necessary to protect the server systems from misuse. The storage of this data takes place only for a temporary period, to the extent necessary to enable the use of our services. The data is not assigned to specific persons and is not merged with data from other data sources. The use of our websites is therefore largely anonymous. This applies until you voluntarily provide us with further data on your own decision, in particular if you register for our courses, our newsletter or contact us via our contact form or by e-mail. The personal data collected in this context includes the data you provide to us, such as your full name, postal address, e-mail address, telephone or telephone address. Fax number and further information about yourself. We may store this data for the purpose of processing your request and in the event that follow-up questions arise. In addition, personal data will only be collected and stored if you have given your express consent.

How does Evolve-Now Academy use my personal data?

We use your personal data primarily to make the use of our websites and services as safe, pleasant, interesting and efficient as possible for you. Furthermore, we use your personal data to answer your inquiries and to send you newsletters and messages as well as to personalize the content of our websites.

Will personal data be passed on to third parties?

Evolve-Now Academy will not in principle pass on, sell or otherwise transfer your personal data to third parties unless you have your express consent, a transfer to third parties is absolutely necessary for the fulfilment of contractual purposes, it serves the protection of our legitimate interests (in particular in the event of violations of the terms of use of our websites) or we are obliged by law or by a court order or a corresponding decision to disclose the personal data. An unauthorized transfer of the data to third parties outside our company is prevented by us in the best possible way with modern security methods.

Security

Evolve-Now Academy uses and develops technical and organizational security measures according to the state of the art in order to protect your personal data provided to us from accidental or intentional manipulation, loss, destruction or access by unauthorized persons. Only employees or other Evolve-Now Academy authorized persons have access to the personal data. These persons are obliged to treat this information confidentially. However, Evolve-Now Academy cannot guarantee, despite these precautions, that third parties will gain unauthorized access to the personal data.

Duration of Evolve-Now Academy's use of data and retention of your data

Evolve-Now Academy deletes or blocks personal data as soon as the purpose of data use has been achieved or has ceased to apply. Any further storage of data will only take place if we are legally obliged to do so. After expiry of applicable legal retention periods, the corresponding data will be routinely deleted or blocked-in accordance with the applicable data protection regulations. If you have consented to the storage, processing and use of your data for a longer period, we will delete or block the data after the end of the respective period or upon revocation of your consent.

You have the right to information about the personal data stored by us, their origin and recipient as well as the purpose of the data processing at any time. Furthermore, you have the right to request the immediate correction of incorrect personal data - also by means of a supplementary declaration - as well as the deletion of personal data.
