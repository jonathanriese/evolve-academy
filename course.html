Evolve Course

The course is delivered online over sixteen weeks through 8 modules. EVOLVE is highly experiential. For each thematic module, we introduce concepts and tools which you customize to fit your personal situation. We also practice mindfulness and relational skills together each time we meet. Throughout the program you will be intimately supported in transferring the learning by a small group of 4 peers. This group meets in structured 1-hour meetings in the weeks in-between sessions, so that there is a weekly impulse for your evolution. Session recordings, practices and other resources will be available on our course website.

Training Modules

The course consists of 8 modules that build logically upon each other.

  1. Life transitions as evolutionary opportunities
    1. Stages and lines of adult development
    2. Navigating life stage transitions
    3. My transition ahead: What do I want to leave behind, what wants to emerge
  2. Understanding my needs, longings and emotions
    1. Map of human needs and longings
    2. Self-assessment of need fulfillment
    3. Five core emotions and link to needs
  3. Assessing my current internal operating system
    1. Reactive vs. creative operating system
    2. My triggers, defenses and needs
    3. Iceberg coaching method
  4. Meeting sticky habits and defensive patterns
    1. The anatomy of habits
    2. Getting to know my inner protectors
    3. Dialoging and negotiating with inner parts
  5. Holding my past with compassion to unleash the future
    1. Feeing up the future through integrating trauma
    2. Principles of trauma integration
    3. Touching on my own past with compassion
  6. Deepening my belonging and relationships
    1. My five circles of belonging
    2. Relational 101: listening, requests and boundaries
    3. The U-turn: from projections to self-compassion
  7. Connecting to my purpose and passion
    1. Purpose at the intersection of my gifts, passion and the world’s needs
    2. Guided journey into the future
    3. Listening to the whisper
  8. Energizing myself for the journey ahead
    1. The energy matrix
    2. My inner compass and map for the journey
    3. Blessings for the journey
